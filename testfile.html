Radiopharmaceuticals

an inspiration for this research

Radiopharmaceuticals for targeting amyloid aggregates are primarily used in positron emission tomography (PET) scans to diagnose conditions like Alzheimer's disease. Approved examples include florbetapir F18 (Amyvid™), florbetaben F18 (Neuraceq™), and flutemetamol F18 (Vizamyl™), which are designed to bind to amyloid plaques in the brain.

  • Low binding inhibition constant (Ki)
  • Low dissociation constants (Kd values of less than 10 nM)
